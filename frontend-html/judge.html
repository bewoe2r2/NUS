Welcome to the Bewo Health Engine -- a comprehensive AI-powered platform that combines Hidden Markov Models, multi-agent intelligence, and clinical decision support to revolutionize proactive healthcare monitoring.

This guided walkthrough will take you through each component of the system, demonstrating how wearable biometric data flows through our ML pipeline to generate real-time clinical insights and actionable recommendations for healthcare professionals.

  • Real-Time Biometric Analysis
    Continuous monitoring of glucose, HRV, sleep, and activity data from wearable devices
  • HMM State Classification
    Viterbi decoding and Baum-Welch training for Stable, Warning, and Crisis states
  • Multi-Agent AI Orchestration
    18 specialized AI tools coordinating clinical analysis, triage, and drug interaction checks
  • SBAR Clinical Reporting
    Structured clinical reports following the gold-standard SBAR communication framework
Why This Matters
"Preventable adverse drug events affect 1.3 million people annually. By combining HMM-based health state prediction with automated drug interaction screening, we can identify at-risk patients before crisis events occur -- shifting healthcare from reactive to truly proactive."
