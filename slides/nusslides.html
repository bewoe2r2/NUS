■ PDPA Compliant Sections 20, 24, 25
■ Data Never Leaves Singapore

4 Core Data Protection Layers
1
PII Firewall Mandatory
Name, NRIC, address, phone, email stripped before any LLM call. AI never sees who the patient is. Regex + NER dual pass.
2
Data Sovereignty
All infrastructure in Singapore region only. HMM runs on-device. No patient data crosses national borders.
3
Immutable Audit Trail
Every AI decision logged: input state, model output, safety gate result, final action. Fully auditable by MOH at any time.
4
6-Month Rolling Retention
Auto-deletion triggers on glucose, HMM states, voice transcripts. PDPA Section 25 compliant.
Ethics & Consent Framework
Informed Consent (Tiered)
BASIC (phone sensors) → ENHANCED (+Fitbit) → PREMIUM (+CGM). Granular opt-in, not all-or-nothing.
Right to Explanation (PDPA S.20)
Every HMM state: which feature, what weight, what probability. Patient asks "why WARNING?" and gets a readable answer.
Human-in-the-Loop
Medication suggestions NEVER auto-applied. Always flagged for doctor review. The system recommends. The human decides.
