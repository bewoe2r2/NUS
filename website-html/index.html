Our Approach

Three Innovation Pillars

HMM Prediction Engine

VITERBI + MONTE CARLO + COUNTERFACTUAL

Three-state clinical model (STABLE / WARNING / CRISIS) with Viterbi decoding on 9 weighted biomarkers. Monte Carlo simulation runs 2,000 future trajectories for 48-hour advance crisis prediction.

Agentic AI Orchestration

18 AUTONOMOUS TOOLS WIRED TO REAL SYSTEMS

Gemini AI reasons over full HMM context and autonomously executes 18 tools: booking appointments, alerting caregivers, adjusting medication schedules. Real actions, not just chat responses.

Behavioral Science Layer

PROSPECT THEORY + NUDGE THEORY

Loss-aversion voucher gamification, adaptive nudge timing learned from patient response patterns, mood-aware conversations, and daily challenges scaled to clinical state.
