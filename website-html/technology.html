How Bewo actually works

A clinically-grounded prediction engine paired with autonomous AI agents that take real-world action. Every component is evidence-based.

Hidden Markov Model

Three clinical states. Nine biomarkers. Real-time state inference.

STABLE

Patient vitals within target range. Proactive wellness maintenance.

Glucose 4.0-7.0 mmol/L Adherence > 80% Steps > 5,000/day HRV > 30ms
WARNING

Early deterioration detected. Preventive intervention triggered.

Glucose 7.0-11.0 mmol/L Declining adherence Reduced activity Sleep disrupted
CRISIS

Immediate clinical attention required. Full escalation activated.

Glucose > 11.0 mmol/L Multiple missed meds HRV below threshold BP elevated

Three Prediction Engines

Layered analysis for maximum clinical accuracy.

Viterbi Decoding

Finds the most likely sequence of hidden states given observed biomarkers. Current state classification with confidence score.

STATE INFERENCE

Monte Carlo Simulation

2,000 stochastic future trajectories over 48h horizon. Quantifies crisis probability and expected time-to-crisis.

2,000 PATHS

Counterfactual Engine

"What if?" Bayesian simulation. Projects how medication, diet, or exercise would alter the crisis trajectory.
