# NUS BAC Innovation Challenge — Past Editions & Winners Research

> **Competition:** NUS Business Analytics Centre (BAC) Annual Innovation Challenge

### 2017 — CFLD-NUS Business Analytics Innovation Challenge (Inaugural)
- **Partner:** China Fortune Land Development (CFLD)
- **Theme:** Real Estate / Land Price Evaluation
- **Problem:** Use data mining and machine learning to evaluate land prices in Kuala Lumpur, New Delhi, Mumbai, or Manila.
- **Participants:** 19 teams from NUS; 8 chosen for finals (held 2 December 2017, ran from 12 Oct – 2 Dec).
- **Winners:**
  - **1st Place: Team Outliers** — Rahul Dey, Raghav Garg, Sudharsan Ganesh Thyagaraj, Ashesh Sasidharan (MSBA students)
  - **2nd Place: Team EzInvest** — Goh Jie Da, Chew Shao Quan, Michelle Goh, Lai Shumin (Year 3 & 4 undergraduate BA students; youngest team)
  - **3rd Place: Team Fantastic Four** — Debosmita Chaudhuri, Rahul Gupta, Mayank Khanna, Swetha Narayanan (MSBA students)
- **Sources:**

### 2022 — NUS-Cargill Analytics Innovation Challenge
- **Partner:** Cargill (international agri-business giant, 155,000 employees in 70 countries)
- **Theme:** Agriculture / Commodity Analytics
- **Problem Statements:**
  1. Model and mitigate the volatility in commodity prices to help Cargill customers optimise buying behaviour.
  2. Automate assessment of grain to improve quality assurance at Cargill.
- **Judging Criteria:** Innovation & feasibility, reliability & quality of models, creative use of data, value created, presentation quality.
- **Prize:** S$5,000 team cash prize + individual trophies.
- **Winners:**
  - **1st Place: Team Multiverse** — Jiang Jinjing, Kyle Asano, Lin Yaqi, Ng Wing Kei Tracy, Ng Zhen Qiang Max, Zhang Jie (all MSBA students). Their solution used **computer vision in the grain quality assaying process**.
  - 2nd and 3rd place details not publicly available.
- **Sources:**
  - [NUS Computing News](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/news/2022-msba-win-nus-cargill-analytics-challenge/)

### 2023 — Huawei-NUS Innovation Challenge
- **Partner:** Huawei International (Singapore subsidiary) + Audaque Data Technology
- **Theme:** Finance / Stock Trading
- **Problem:** Build a stock trading model based on historical transaction data to allow users to "buy low, sell high" at optimal timing.
- **Prizes:** Grand Prize S$10,000 | 2nd Prize S$5,000 | 3rd Prize S$3,000
- **Key Milestone:** First year the challenge was opened to students from ALL Singapore universities and polytechnics (previously NUS-only). Also marked NUS BAC's 10th anniversary.
- **Platform:** HUAWEI CLOUD ModelArts (one-stop AI development platform).
- **Winners:**
  - MSBA students clinched top spots ("a clean sweep"). Specific team names not publicly detailed in search results.
- **Sources:**

### 2025 — NUS–Guru Network AI Innovation Challenge
- **Partner:** Guru Network (Web3/AI company)
- **Theme:** GenAI Solutions for Business Innovation (open-ended — projects spanned travel, healthcare/productivity, and database technology)
- **Prizes:** S$70,000–75,000 total prize pool. Over 6,500 public votes cast. Top 8 teams made finals.
- **Winners:**
  - **Champion (tied): Team AskNgo** (NUS MSBA, NUS SoC & NTU) — built an **AI-powered travel companion** 🏆
  - **Champion (tied): Team Jiaye** (NUS MSBA) 🏆
  - **2nd Runner-Up: TheAlgorithmicallyChallenged** (NUS MSBA & NUS SoC, including Darrell Goh)
- **Notable projects from finalists:** AI-powered travel companions, ADHD productivity tools, and advanced database querying solutions.
- **Sources:**
  - [NUS Computing](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/bytes/2-champion-teams-crowned-at-the-guru-network-ai-innovation-challenge-2025-finals/)

### 2026 — NUS–Synapxe–IMDA AI Innovation Challenge (10th Edition, Current)
- **Partners:** Synapxe (national healthtech agency) + IMDA (Infocomm Media Development Authority)
- **Theme:** Healthcare / Chronic Disease Management — "Empower Patients, Enable Community, Elevate Healthcare"
- **Problem:** Build AI-powered solutions that identify at-risk individuals before symptoms appear. Focus areas: early identification of at-risk individuals, timely screening & diagnosis, continuous remote monitoring, assisting patients in managing health from home.
- **Target conditions:** Diabetes, hypertension, and high lipid levels (affecting ~1.8 million Singaporeans).
- **Special Award:** NMLP Special Award (S$5,000) for best use of SEA-LION and MERaLiON AI models.
- **New this year:** Junior college students can participate alongside university and polytechnic teams.
- **Status:** Registration closed 15 Feb 2026. Competition ongoing.
- **Sources:**
  - [Synapxe Media Release](https://www.synapxe.sg/media-releases/artificial-intelligence/ai-innovation-challenge-nus-imda)
  - [IMDA Press Release](https://www.imda.gov.sg/resources/press-releases-factsheets-and-speeches/press-releases/2026/ai-solutions-combating-chronic-diseases)
  - [Official Website](https://www.sg-innovationchallenge.org/)

## Notes
- NUS BAC has organized annual business analytics competitions since 2014, though the numbered "Innovation Challenge" series started around 2016.
- The competition was NUS-only until 2023, when it expanded to all Singapore universities and polytechnics.
- In 2026, it further expanded to include junior college students.
- Many detailed winner lists are hosted behind NUS MSBA's website (msba.nus.edu.sg) which is not easily scraped. For complete winner details, check those pages directly.
- The official challenge website (sg-innovationchallenge.org) is a React single-page app whose content is not indexed by search engines.
